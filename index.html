Budget Review

  • Budget spreadsheet: Ellie shared an updated budget. We currently have $380 in the committee fund. She noted we need to keep expenses under $140 to ensure a reasonable profit margin.

New Business

Venue Confirmation

Library walk spot 1975 is confirmed for the event.

Menu Items & Pricing

The committee discussed which items to sell. After some debate, the following menu was agreed upon:

  • Chocolate chip cookies — $1.50 each
  • Brownies — $2.00 each
  • Cupcakes (chocolate) — $2.50 each

Bridget suggested we also offer a "baker's bundle" — any 3 items for $4.00 — to encourage larger purchases. The board unanimously agreed to this idea.

Role Assignments

Zay will recruit at least 8 volunteers for the event. Roles include:

  • Baking crew (prep the night before)
  • Setup crew (morning of the event)
  • Sales table staff
  • Cleanup crew

Marketing & Outreach

Zay proposed making flyers and posting on social media. Bridget asked Daniel to have draft flyers ready by April 17th for review.

Miscellaneous Comments / Questions / Concerns

  • Miguel asked if we need to get food handling permits. Zay will look into this and report back by the next meeting.
  • Ellie raised a concern about allergen labeling. The committee agreed that every item must have a label listing common allergens (nuts, dairy, gluten, eggs).
  • Zay mentioned that a local bakery might donate supplies. He will reach out to them this week.
